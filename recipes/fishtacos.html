Fish Tacos:

Description:

These tacos use an easy to buy tilapia, which can be purchased cheaply at many grocery stores. With the right ingredients they make a delicous and simple fish taco.

Incredients:

  • 3-4 tilapia fillets
  • fresh tortillas
  • finely chopped lettuce
  • queso fresco
  • limes

Steps:

  1. Pan sear tilapia fillets
  2. chop each fillet into strips
  3. place 2 strips into each torilla
  4. crumble queso fresco over taco
  5. add lettuce and squeeze lime
  6. ENJOY!!
