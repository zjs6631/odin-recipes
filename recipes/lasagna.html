Lasagna:

Description

This is an alternative take on the classic lasagna by allowing the pasta to be replaced with thinly cut strips of squash to cut back on carbs.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 lbs of ground turkey meat
  • 3-4 good sized squash
  • parmesean cheese
  • shredded italian blend cheese
  • salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. Brown the turkey meat in a pan
  2. Season the meat to taste with salt and pepper
  3. use a veggie peeler to peel thin strips
  4. layer a baking dish with strips of veggy
  5. add cheese and meat between each layer
  6. add a hefty amount of all cheese to top
  7. bake at 350F for an hour
  8. let rest for 15 mins afterwards
  9. ENJOY!!!
