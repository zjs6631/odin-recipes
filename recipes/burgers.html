Burgers:

Description:

Simple but delicious. You can't go wrong with a burger!

ingredients

  • ground turkey meat
  • 1 egg
  • saltine crackers
  • sliced pepperjack cheese
  • buns

Steps:

  1. mix meat, egg, and crackers in a bowl
  2. form patties using the mixture
  3. place patties in pan on stove
  4. once one side has been browned flip and add cheese
  5. continue cooking
  6. add to bun
  7. ENJOY!!
